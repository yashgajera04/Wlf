Frequently Asked Questions

Section I - Sign in & Register

Please access Sony LIV at https://www.sonyliv.com/

You can sign in using your mobile number or email on the Sony LIV app or website.

Currently, Sony LIV services are available only within India and select regions.

Open Sony LIV on your TV, go to Sign In, and enter the code displayed on your TV screen on sonyliv.com/device/activate.

Section II - Platform Support

Android 5.0 or above, iOS 12 or above are required for Sony LIV app compatibility.

Older Samsung TV models are not supported due to system limitations and app performance requirements.

Section III - Billing & Subscriptions

You can subscribe on the Sony LIV website or via the mobile app. Choose a plan, provide payment details (credit/debit card, UPI, or supported wallets) and confirm the purchase.

Go to Account > Subscriptions in the app or website and select Cancel. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

Refunds depend on the payment method and local policies. In most cases, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis — contact support with your order details for assistance.

We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, and selected mobile wallet providers. Available options may vary by country.

Section IV - Playback & Troubleshooting

Buffering usually indicates a slow or unstable internet connection. Try switching to a faster network, lowering the playback quality in the player settings, or restarting your router.

During playback, open the subtitles/CC menu in the player and enable the desired language. If subtitles are missing for a title, they may not be available for that content.

Try clearing the app cache, updating to the latest app version, or reinstalling the app. If the issue persists, note the error message and contact support with device details.

Ensure you have a stable, high-speed connection and check player quality settings. On some plans, higher-quality streams may require a premium subscription.

Section V - Plans & Offers

Plans vary by price, streaming quality, number of simultaneous devices, and availability of premium content. Check the Subscribe page for current plan details and features.

Yes — promotional offers and voucher codes are offered periodically. Apply codes at checkout. Terms and validities differ per offer.

Free trial availability changes over time and may be region-specific. Check the Subscribe page or promotional banners for active trials.

Section VI - Contact & Support

You can contact us via the Contact Us page, in-app chat, or by email. For account/payment issues, include your account email/phone and transaction details for faster resolution.

Response times vary by channel and volume. In-app chat and ticket responses are typically faster; urgent issues are prioritized where possible.

Use the Report option on the content page or contact support with the title, timestamp and reason. Our team will review the report and take action if needed.
